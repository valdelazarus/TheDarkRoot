The Dark Root

Game Description

You play the role of Bobbie, a laid-back adventurer. He was looking for inspiration, but a sudden event happened...

Hang in there, adventurer!

How To Play

W -> Move Up

A -> Move Left

S -> Move Down

D -> Move Right

Left Click -> Normal Shoot

Right Click -> Special Shoot
