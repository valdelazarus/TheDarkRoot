How To Play

Movement

W: Move Up

A: Move Left

S: Move Down

D: Move Right

General Attack


Left Click: Normal Shoot

Right Click: Special Shoot

Switch Weapons

1: Normal default weapon

2: Small weapon (Ammo Required)

3: Large weapon (Ammo Required)
