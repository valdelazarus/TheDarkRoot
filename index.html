The Dark Root

Game Description

Your goal is to escape the dark dungeon.
How to win: Stay alive and defeat all bosses to advance through all levels.
Game over condition: Player loses all health points.

How to play:

 W -> Move Up
 A -> Move Left
 S -> Move Down
 D -> Move Right
 Left Click -> Normal shot
 Right Click -> Especial Shot
