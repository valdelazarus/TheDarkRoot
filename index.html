The Dark Root

Game Description

You play the role of Bobbie, a laid-back adventurer. He was looking for inspiration, but a sudden event happened...

Hang in there, adventurer!

How To Play

Movement:

W -> Move Up

A -> Move Left

S -> Move Down

D -> Move Right

General Attack:

Left Click -> Normal Shoot

Right Click -> Special Shoot

Change to selected weapon if there is ammo available:

1 -> Normal default weapon

2 -> Small weapon

3 -> Large weapon
